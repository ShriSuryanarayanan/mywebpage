All about us!

In this website, we put all the bits of cricket from all the nooks and crannies of the world together. We provide you with live scorecards, news upto 30 days old, a page for all those small and unrecognised cricket leagues, a page for all the well known and well loved leagues, a collaborative area where all of you can start a discussion and digitally speak with like minded people, online shopping links, ticket buying links and lastly the most exciting bit, team sheets from all the major icc cricket teams. Our aim is to bring cricket from the stadium and into the homes of everyone and even if you are not a cricket fan, you will surely love this.

How to play the sport??

Playing the sport is actually really simple... unless you want to play professionally but first let's get to the simple rules. There are three main formats of cricket: test, T20I and ODI. These are determined by the number of overs in each match.
